Website topic: Eco friendly living

Points to note:

-Participants are required to design a user friendly , functional,
creative, and informative website .

-All links, buttons, and navigation must work properly.

-Assessment will be based on following criteria:

	-Innovative idea and approach

	-Originality and creativity

	-Practical and education value

	-Presentation and demonstration

The students will have to explain their project to the judges
